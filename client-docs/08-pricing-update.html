Pricing Update 2026

Updated solo freelancer rates based on current market research — March 2026

Internal
Why update prices? Current rates are below the solo freelancer market floor ($1,000–$5,000 for small business sites). At $499 for a Starter, you are pricing below your own time value. A 15-hour build at a modest $65/hr = $975 minimum. These updated prices are still on the conservative end for a solo — not agency rates.

02 Add-Ons & Optional Services
Service Old Price New Price What it involves
Domain Purchase Setup $35 $50–$75 Research, register, configure DNS, connect to hosting, transfer account. ~45–60 min.
Domain Renewal Management $45/yr $60–$75/yr Track expiry, send reminders, process renewal on client approval. Minimal effort, recurring income.
Content Migration Custom quote $100–$300 Moving content from old site. Lower end = simple/small. Upper end = messy or large. Quote only if truly complex.
Rush / Expedited Delivery +25% +25–30% Keep Rearranging schedule, working faster/nights/weekends. The % scales automatically with project size — good structure.
Hosting Setup / Config Not listed Included in all packages Upload files, configure Netlify/GitHub Pages/cPanel, SSL cert, test. Part of delivering a finished site — not a separate charge. Only billable if client switches hosts after launch.
Google Business Profile Setup Not listed $75–$100 New Claim/verify listing, add photos, hours, services, link to site. ~45–60 min. High value to local clients.
Logo Design Not listed $150–$250 New Subcontract to Fiverr (~$50), mark up, handle client revisions. You manage, they execute.
Copywriting (per page) Not listed $75–$125/page New Subcontract to a writer (~$30–50/page), review, fit to design. Clients who can't write their own content will pay for this.
03 Post-Launch Changes
Change Type Old Price New Price What it involves
Small Change (pay-per) $50 $65–$75 Text swap, image swap, color tweak, typo fix. Under 30 min.
Medium Change (pay-per) $150 $175–$200 New section, form update, layout modification. 1–3 hours.
Large Change (pay-per) $300+ $350–$400+ New page, new feature, third-party integration. 4–8+ hours.
Medium Change (during support) $150 $175 Same as above — billable even within support window for scope additions.
Large Change (during support) $300+ $350+ New pages, new features — clearly outside included support scope.
Monthly Retainer $150/mo (3 small changes) $175–$200/mo Predictable recurring income. Covers 3 small changes, priority response, monthly health check. Medium/large billed separately.
Late fees: $50/week + 1.5%/mo interest is solid and professional — keep this exactly as is.
