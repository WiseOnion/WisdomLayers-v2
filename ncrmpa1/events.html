Chapter Events

Annual Events

Beyond our monthly meetings, Chapter One hosts signature events throughout the year.

Annual Picnic

A beloved summer tradition bringing together chapter members, families, and friends for a day of fellowship, food, and fun. Typically held in late summer — date announced at chapter meetings.

Veterans Day Ceremony

Chapter One proudly participates in and hosts Veterans Day ceremonies honoring all who served. A solemn and meaningful tribute to the sacrifices of Military Police veterans across all eras.

Holiday Gathering

An annual end-of-year celebration celebrating the brotherhood, the year's accomplishments, and the season. Members and families come together to close out the year in fellowship and gratitude.
