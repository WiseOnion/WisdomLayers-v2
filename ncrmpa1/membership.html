Membership Open to All Military Police — Active, Discharged & Retired

Who Can Join

Open to All Who Served

RMPA Chapter One welcomes all individuals who have served honorably in the Military Police Corps. We believe that every person who wore the MP brassard deserves a community that understands their sacrifice, their service, and their ongoing commitment to honor and brotherhood.

Active Duty Military Police

Currently serving MPs are welcome to join and build connections ahead of transition.

Honorably Discharged Veterans

All veterans who received an honorable discharge from Military Police duty are eligible.

Retired Military Police

All retired MP personnel of any rank, branch, or era of service are proudly welcomed.

No Barriers, No Exceptions

Open regardless of race, color, sex, religion, age, disability, or national origin.

Why Join

Member Benefits

Everything you gain by becoming part of RMPA Chapter One — a community built on shared service.

01

Brotherhood

Connect with hundreds of active, discharged, and retired Military Police across North Carolina and the nation. The bond forged in uniform lasts a lifetime.

02

Resources

Access member-exclusive information on VA benefits, veteran services, legal resources, career transition support, and more — curated for the MP community.

03

Monthly Meetings

Participate in monthly chapter meetings at the John D. Fuller Sr. Complex — every 1st Saturday at 9:00 AM. Stay informed, engaged, and connected year-round.

04

Veteran Support

Tap into a network that advocates for your veteran benefits, supports peer mental health, and ensures no former Military Police soldier goes without community.

Get Started

Apply for Membership

Completing the form on the right submits your membership interest to Chapter One officers. We'll review your application and reach out personally to welcome you into the brotherhood.

Annual Dues

Chapter One membership is sustained by annual dues assessed during the January meeting each year. Dues amounts are established by the chapter and communicated to all members at the annual business meeting.

Checks should be made payable to: RMPA Chapter 1

Dues may also be addressed at any monthly meeting. Contact the Treasurer, MSG (Ret) Jeffrey Zack, for current dues structure and payment options at zackj@faytechcc.edu.
