About the Association

Who
We Are

The Retired Military Police Association (RMPA), North Carolina Chapter One, is dedicated to fostering unity among active, honorably discharged, and retired Military Police. Our goal is to build a strong community rooted in camaraderie, support, and shared purpose. We welcome all individuals who share our mission, regardless of race, color, sex, religion, age, disability, or national origin.

Strengthen Connections

Build lasting bonds among Military Police members, past and present, across every era of service.

Promote Friendship & Respect

A culture of mutual support, dignity, and brotherhood across all backgrounds and service branches.

Support Law Enforcement

Standing behind active and retired Military Police and the broader Civilian Law Enforcement community.

Honor the Legacy

Enhancing the recognition and reputation of the Military Police Corps and those who served with distinction.
