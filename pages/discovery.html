Discovery Questionnaire

Help us understand your business and vision before your discovery call. The more detail you share, the better we can serve you.

Before We Begin Building

This questionnaire helps WisdomLayers understand your business, goals, and vision before the discovery call. Completing it in advance ensures our session is focused and productive. There are no wrong answers — answer as thoroughly as you can. Incomplete answers are fine; we will cover the rest during the call.

Client Information

1

Your Business

Business Overview

What do you do, and what makes you different from competitors?
Describe the type of person most likely to buy from you (age range, location, profession, lifestyle, problem they have, etc.)
Describe in your own words what you want the site to accomplish.
2

Website Scope

Pages and Features

List every page you want (e.g., Home, About, Services, Portfolio, Contact, Blog, FAQ, etc.)
List any functionality beyond standard pages. Some features may require third-party tools or additional cost.
Let us know what exists and what still needs to be written or created.
3

Brand and Design

Visual Identity

If you do not have hex codes, describe the colors or the feeling you want (e.g., professional dark navy with gold accents).
These do not need to be in your industry — just sites whose look and feel appeals to you. This is one of the most helpful things you can provide.
Think about words like: modern, minimal, bold, elegant, warm, playful, professional, luxury, corporate, casual...
4

Competition and Market

Competitive Landscape

Include their website URLs if possible. This helps us understand the market standard and how to position your site.
Design quality, clarity of services, stronger call to action, mobile experience, trust signals, etc.
5

Domain and Hosting

Technical Setup

If so, provide the URL and describe what you want to keep, improve, or completely replace.
6

Social Media and Online Presence

Online Profiles

List all active social media profiles to be linked or integrated. Leave blank if you do not have that platform.

7

Anything Else

Final Notes

Any unique requirements, constraints, special requests, or context we should know before we begin.
When you look at the finished website, what would make you say "this is exactly what I wanted"?

Note: You do not need to answer every question perfectly. Bring your best effort and we will clarify anything during the discovery call. The more detail you provide, the more focused and productive our session will be.
