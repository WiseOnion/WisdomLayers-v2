Our Story

Real Brooklyn
Flavor Here

Brooklyn Pizzeria brings the real taste of New York-style pizza to Fayetteville, NC. We specialize in thin, crispy, hand-tossed pies made with quality ingredients and that unmistakable Brooklyn attitude.

Whether you're sitting down for dinner, calling in a carry-out order, or having us cater your next event, every pie is made fresh with the same care and the same recipes. Dough tossed by hand. Sauce made the right way. Cheese layered generously.

Find us on Ramsey Street, Suite 106. We're open seven days a week, and we're always ready to make your pizza exactly the way you want it.
