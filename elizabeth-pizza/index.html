Our Story

A Family Tradition Since 1984

Elizabeth's Pizza on Skibo Road is an authentic Italian restaurant that has been family owned and operated for over 30 years. Our first Fayetteville location opened in 1984, and we've been serving the community with the same love and dedication ever since.

We're not a franchise — we're a family. A family of related restaurants sharing similar menus and values, built around a dedication to traditional Italian cuisine and a family-oriented atmosphere that keeps our guests coming back generation after generation.

From our hand-tossed New York style pizza to our pasta dishes made with sauces crafted from scratch, everything on our menu is prepared with the care and quality that has defined Elizabeth's for over four decades.
