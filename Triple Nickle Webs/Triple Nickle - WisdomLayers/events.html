Where Your Ticket Goes

Events That Fund a Mission

Scholarship Fund

The primary beneficiary of every extravaganza is our annual scholarship program — 48 awards per year to students across 12 schools.

  • 48 scholarships annually
  • 12 Cumberland & Harnett County schools
  • Targeting 2.5–3.0 GPA students
  • Single-parent/income households

Historical Preservation

Events fund our ongoing work to document, preserve, and share the story of the 555th with schools, museums, and the public.

  • Educational presentations
  • Historical archive maintenance
  • Commemorative ceremonies
  • Memorial Day & Veterans Day events

Community Outreach

Our events sustain the chapter's ability to serve the broader Fayetteville and Fort Liberty community throughout the year.

  • Veteran family support
  • Chapter operations
  • National 555th PIA programs
  • JROTC cadet support

Can't Make It to an Event?
