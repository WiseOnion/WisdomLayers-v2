Membership

Join us in preserving the legacy of the Triple Nickles

Become a Member

Open to All

Membership in the Samuel Council Chapter is open to all without regard to race, gender, or ethnicity — just as the 555th stood for inclusion before the Army required it.

By joining, you become part of a mission to preserve one of America's most important and least-told military stories, support scholarship recipients in Cumberland County, and honor those who served.

To join, submit your contact information and a chapter representative will follow up with you directly.

Submit an Interest Form

By submitting your contact details, you are providing your data to the Samuel Council Chapter 555th PIA who may contact you.
