What Happens Next

1

Pay Your Deposit

You'll receive an invoice for 50% of your project total. Once payment is received, we'll move to step 2 within 24 hours.

2

Client Portal Access

You'll get access to your personalized Client Portal where you can track progress, upload content, and communicate with me throughout the project.

3

Discovery Call (30-45 minutes)

You'll provide all necessary materials (text, images, logos) through your Client Portal. The faster you provide content, the faster we can launch!

5

Development Begins

I'll start building your website with regular check-ins and progress updates. You'll have opportunities to provide feedback throughout.

7

Final Payment & Launch

After receiving your final payment (remaining 50%), your website goes live and your support period begins!

What You Need to Prepare

Content Checklist

Text Content: All copy for your pages (Home, About, Services, Contact, etc.). Write it in a Google Doc or Word file.
High-Quality Images: Professional photos or graphics (minimum 1920px width recommended). Stock photos can work if needed.
Logo: Your business logo in high resolution (PNG with transparent background preferred).
Brand Colors: Your preferred color palette (hex codes if you have them, or examples from other sites you like).
Domain & Hosting: If you haven't purchased these yet, I can help! (See optional services in your agreement)
Examples You Like: 3-5 websites you admire (design inspiration helps me understand your vision).

Pro Tip: Content Preparation

The #1 thing that speeds up or slows down projects is content. If you have your text and images ready before we start, we can move much faster! Don't worry if you need help - I'll guide you through it.

Communication Guidelines

How We'll Stay in Touch

  • Email: Best for detailed questions, content submissions, and formal approvals
  • Client Portal: Track progress, view updates, and upload files
  • Video Calls: Scheduled for discovery, major milestones, and final review
  • Response Time: I typically respond within 24 hours on business days (Monday-Friday)

Important: Please respond to feedback requests within 5-10 business days to keep your project on track. If I don't hear from you for 30+ days after two contact attempts, the project may be paused or terminated per our agreement.

Your Support Period

After launch, you get FREE support for:

  • Bug fixes (broken links, display issues, functionality problems)
  • Small text updates (phone numbers, hours, addresses, typos)
  • Image swaps (replacing existing images with same-size images)
  • Minor styling adjustments

Support Duration:

  • Starter: 1 month post-launch
  • Professional: 2 months post-launch
  • Premium: 3 months post-launch
  • Custom: Up to 6 months (as agreed)

After your support period, you can choose pay-per-change ($50/$150/$300) or monthly retainer ($150/month). We'll discuss options before your support expires.
